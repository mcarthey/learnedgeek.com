### 🔴 Page Speed & Performance — High Impact

**Finding:** Mobile page speed score needs improvement (55/100) — Google uses page speed as a direct ranking factor

**Finding:** Google's own SEO audit scores this page at 72/100 — there are technical SEO issues Google has flagged directly


### 🔴 Title Tag — High Impact

**Finding:** Title is too short (10 chars; aim for 50-60)


### 🔴 Local Business Schema — High Impact

**Finding:** No structured data (schema markup) found — this helps Google display rich results and understand your business type


### 🔴 Geographic Keywords — High Impact

**Finding:** No geographic location keyword in the page title

**Finding:** No geographic keyword in the main heading (H1)


### 🟡 Google Business Profile — Medium Impact

**Finding:** Only 7 Google review(s) — businesses with 10+ reviews see significantly better local pack visibility

**Finding:** Average rating is 3.8/5.0 — ratings below 4.0 negatively affect click-through rates and local pack rankings; a review response strategy is worth discussing

**Finding:** Only 2 photo(s) on the Google Business Profile — aim for at least 10 photos

**Finding:** No business description on the Google Business Profile — a well-written description with relevant keywords improves both rankings and conversions


### 🟡 Meta Description — Medium Impact

**Finding:** Missing meta description


### 🟡 Heading Structure — Medium Impact

**Finding:** No H2 tags found


### 🟡 Canonical Tags — Medium Impact

**Finding:** No canonical tag found — without it, Google may index duplicate versions of your pages and split your ranking authority


### 🟡 Image Optimization — Medium Impact

**Finding:** 4 images missing alt text — alt text helps search engines understand your images


### 🟢 Social Media Tags — Low Impact

**Finding:** Missing Open Graph tags: og:title, og:description, og:image

## Suggested Scope of Work

Based on the findings above, here is a summary of recommended improvements:

- Rewrite and optimize page title tags with geographic keywords
- Write compelling meta descriptions for key pages
- Implement LocalBusiness structured data markup
- Integrate geographic keywords into titles, headings, and content
- Audit and address page speed issues (images, caching, etc.)
- Restructure page headings for proper H1/H2 hierarchy
- Add descriptive alt text to all images
- Add canonical tags to prevent duplicate content issues
- Add Open Graph tags for improved social media sharing
- Audit and optimize Google Business Profile listing
- Write keyword-rich Google Business Profile description
- Add professional photos to Google Business Profile
- Implement a review generation strategy for Google
