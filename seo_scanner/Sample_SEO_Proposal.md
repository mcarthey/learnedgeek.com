### 🔴 Title Tag — High Impact

**Finding:** Title is too short (12 chars; aim for 50–60)


### 🔴 Local Business Schema — High Impact

**Finding:** No structured data (schema markup) found — this helps Google display rich results and understand your business type


### 🔴 NAP Consistency — High Impact

**Finding:** No phone number detected on this page — for local businesses, your phone number should appear on every page

**Finding:** No street address detected — displaying your address consistently is critical for local search rankings (NAP consistency)


### 🔴 Geographic Keywords — High Impact

**Finding:** No geographic location keyword in the page title

**Finding:** No geographic keyword in the main heading (H1)


### 🟡 Meta Description — Medium Impact

**Finding:** Missing meta description


### 🟡 Heading Structure — Medium Impact

**Finding:** No H2 tags found — subheadings help structure content for search engines


### 🟡 Canonical Tags — Medium Impact

**Finding:** No canonical tag found — without it, Google may index duplicate versions of your pages and split your ranking authority


### 🟡 Image Optimization — Medium Impact

**Finding:** 5 images missing alt text — alt text helps search engines understand your images


### 🟢 Social Media Tags — Low Impact

**Finding:** Missing Open Graph tags: og:title, og:description, og:image — these control how your page appears when shared on social media and can affect click-through rates

## Suggested Scope of Work

Based on the findings above, here is a summary of recommended improvements:

- Rewrite and optimize page title tags
- Write compelling meta descriptions for key pages
- Implement LocalBusiness structured data markup
- Ensure Name, Address, and Phone appear consistently site-wide
- Integrate geographic keywords into titles, headings, and page content
- Audit and address page speed issues (image optimization, caching, etc.)
- Restructure page headings for proper H1/H2 hierarchy
- Add descriptive alt text to all images
- Add canonical tags to prevent duplicate content issues
- Add Open Graph tags for improved social media sharing
